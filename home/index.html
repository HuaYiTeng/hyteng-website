FACTORY QUALITY CONTROL

Strict 5-Step Quality Assurance Process

At Hua Yi Teng, we do not leave safety to chance. Our ISO9001:2015 facility utilizes automated test equipment (ATE) and strict processes to secure flawless reliability for your devices.

01

IQC (Raw Material Inspection)

Strict incoming inspection of ICs, transformers, and raw components under rigorous AQL standards.

02

SMT & ATE Scanning

100% automated optical inspection and param electrical scanning during the assembly line.

03

4-Hour Burn-In Test

100% of our power adapters undergo a minimum 4-hour, full-load burn-in aging test to eliminate infant mortality.

04

Hi-Pot Isolation Test

100% high-voltage insulation test (AC 3000V) between AC inputs and DC outputs to ensure absolute end-user safety.

05

OQC Verification

Pre-shipment final aesthetics, barcode scan, and compliance packing inspection before shipping.
